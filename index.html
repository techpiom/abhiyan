    Products

    AminoKind-SN Infusion

    The infusion works by providing a ready-to-use mixture of amino acids (the fundamental "salts" or building blocks of protein) directly into the bloodstream.

    Protein Synthesis:

    The amino acids are immediately available for the body's cells to synthesize new proteins, which are essential for repairing damaged tissue (like after surgery or injury), building muscle, producing enzymes and hormones, and supporting immune function.

    Correcting Nitrogen Balance

    The primary goal is to correct a Negative Nitrogen Balance, a state where the body is breaking down more protein than it is building up (catabolism). Providing these amino acids helps shift the body back into an anabolic (building up) state.

    Energy Provision

    Sorbitol or other carbohydrate sources are included to provide energy, preventing the body from breaking down the supplied amino acids for fuel.

    VOL-HES 3%: Essential Plasma Volume Expander

    VOL-HES 3% is a trusted plasma volume expander, indicated for the treatment and prevention of hypovolemia (low blood volume) due to acute blood loss or other conditions. Its active ingredient, Hydroxyethyl Starch (Pentastarch 3%), helps to restore and maintain adequate circulatory volume, ensuring vital organ perfusion in critical situations.

    Effective Plasma Volume Expansion:

    Designed to provide reliable and sustained expansion of intravascular volume.

    Sterile & Non-Pyrogenic

    Manufactured under stringent quality controls to ensure maximum patient safety.

    Single-Dose Container:

    Convenient 500 ml packaging for ready-to-use application in clinical settings.

    Each 20 ml x 1 single-dose container ensures ease of use and reduces the risk of contamination in clinical settings.

    AMINOKIND Infusion: Essential Amino Acid Support for Critical Nutrition

    AMINOKIND Infusion is a sterile, non-pyrogenic solution of pure crystalline amino acids, specifically formulated for intravenous administration. It is designed to provide essential nutritional support for patients who cannot meet their protein requirements orally or enterally, or those in catabolic states requiring supplemental amino acids for tissue repair and physiological function.

    Pure Crystalline Amino Acids:

    Delivers readily available amino acids, the building blocks of protein, crucial for recovery, tissue regeneration, and immune function.

    Nutritive Support:

    Essential for patients experiencing malnutrition, surgical recovery, severe illness, burns, trauma, or other conditions leading to protein depletion.

    Sterile & Non-Pyrogenic:

    Manufactured under strict quality controls to ensure patient safety and purity.

    Convenient Packaging:

    Each 20 ml x 1 single-dose container ensures ease of use and reduces the risk of contamination in clinical settings.
